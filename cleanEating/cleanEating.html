Healthy Eating Tips

  • Parents, moniter what your children eat
  • Eat breakfast everyday and never skip meals
  • Turn off electronics during meals
  • Drink nonfat milk with meals and water with snacks; avoid sugary drinks
  • Choose healthy snacks and limit them to two per day
  • Cut down on saturated fats and sugar
  • Always shop with a list
  • Eat your vegetables first
  • Eat from smaller plates
  • Pay attention to the recommended portions for your age down below and track your calories!
